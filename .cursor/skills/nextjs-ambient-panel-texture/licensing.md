# Preset SVG licensing and provenance

The **33 files** under `bundled-patterns/` (`pattern-1.svg` … `pattern-33.svg`) are tileable chat-background masks extracted from **Telegram for Android 5.14.0** (see [the extraction write-up](https://blog.1a23.com/2020/02/13/telegram-chat-backgrounds-patterns-extracted/)).

They are **Telegram’s visual IP**. Shipping them in a third-party client or design tool is the same class of decision as shipping them in an alternate Telegram client: **verify your product’s licensing and risk tolerance** before distributing them to end users.

This skill bundles them **only** so integrators can prototype quickly. You may replace the directory with your own SVG masks (same naming convention or adjust the preset table in code).
